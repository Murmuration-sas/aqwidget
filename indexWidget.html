The World Health Organization defines guidelines for the main air pollutants. Exceedance of the air quality guideline (AQG) levels is associated with important risks to public health. Besides the AQG value, the World Health Organization defines interim targets (IT). The aim is to provide intermediate objectives to countries that have a hard time complying with the strict guideline. [from the 2021 WHO global air quality guidelines]
